# Brand — The Almanac

A human-friendly overview of the evanharmon.com brand. For the exhaustive,
build-facing spec see [`DESIGN.md`](../../DESIGN.md); for the live reference, visit
**`/brand`** on the site.

## The idea

**The Almanac** treats a personal website like a finely-printed object — equal
parts engineer's notebook and 18th-century broadsheet. Think aged paper, woodcut
engraving, gilt ornaments, and a tactile printed grain, rendered with a dry,
literate wit.

## Personality & voice

- **Tone:** crafted · antiquarian · tactile · confident · quiet.
- **Voice:** literate and wry, leaning on an archaic register — "Send a word,"
  "Elsewhere on the machines," "The full account" — but never at the expense of
  clarity. The house tagline: _"Cantankerous contraptions & philosophical piffle."_
- **Avoid:** startup-speak, emoji as decoration, neon gradients, floating
  drop-shadow cards, and sans-serif body text.

## Logo & monogram

- **Wordmark:** "Evan Harmon" set in Cormorant Garamond (600).
- **Monogram / brand-mark:** the **EH** in Old Standard TT inside a gilt circle
  with a double inset keyline — used at 32 px in the header and as the favicon.
- Keep clear space of at least the height of the "E" around the wordmark.

## Colour

Two themes, one accent each. **Parchment** (light) is the brand-primary palette
with a deep garden-green accent; **Midnight** (dark) carries a vivid gold accent
and is the site's default. The accent is reserved for emphasis — eyebrows,
numerals, links, ornaments — never body text. The green/blue "colour-blocks"
(hero and contact) are the only filled surfaces.

## Typography

Four antique serifs, each with one job: **Cormorant Garamond** (display &
wordmark), **EB Garamond** (body & small-caps labels), **Old Standard TT** (the
monogram), and **Playfair Display** italic (numerals — "No. 1," catalog numbers).

## Motifs

Numbered sections running up the left margin like a book spine; engraved dividers
(`❧ — ❖ — ❧`); gilt ornaments (the heart `❦`, diamond `❖`, leaf `❧`); and a fine
printed grain laid over everything that **scrolls with the page** like ink on
paper. Depth is drawn with engraved inset keylines, not soft shadows.

## Assets & licensing

All four fonts are OFL (self-hosted); icons are Lucide (ISC); the Flammarion
engraving on `/now` is public domain; the EH monogram is type-set. Everything
shipped permits commercial use.
